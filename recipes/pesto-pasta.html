Pesto Pasta Recipe

Description

Pesto pasta is a simple yet flavorful Italian dish featuring pasta, typically spaghetti, linguine, or penne, tossed with pesto sauce. The pesto sauce is made from fresh basil leaves, garlic, pine nuts, Parmesan cheese, and olive oil, blended together into a vibrant green, aromatic sauce. This dish highlights the fresh, herbal notes of basil and the rich, nutty flavor of pine nuts, often garnished with a sprinkle of Parmesan and a drizzle of olive oil for added richness. It's a light, refreshing meal perfect for any time of year.

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) package bow tie pasta
  • 1 teaspoon olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 skinless, boneless chicken breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 pinch crushed red pepper flakes, or to taste
  • ½ cup pesto sauce
  • ⅓ cup oil-packed sun-dried tomatoes, drained and cut into strips

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook until al dente, 8 to 10 minutes; drain.
  3. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Sauté garlic until tender.
  4. Stir in chicken and season with red pepper flakes. Cook until chicken is golden and cooked through.
  5. Combine pasta, chicken, pesto, and sun-dried tomatoes in a large bowl; toss to coat evenly.
